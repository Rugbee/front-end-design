Rugbee

A project collaboration tool for Agile and Lean teams.

Light, fast, and discussion-centered.

Currently in private beta...

Initiate a new project in seconds.

Start contributing visuals, links, discussion and more.

Visuals and other artifacts automatically become hashtags and potential discussion topics or their own sub-projects.

Others referenced in your discussion get notified that they've been mentioned. If they are not already part of the project team, they get an invitation to join.

No need to keep signing in to the app. Particate via email, SMS, or chat.

Create ad-hoc sub-projects from any hashtag or topic.
